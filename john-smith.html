OFFICIAL RULING

Medical Determination: Subject experienced chronic psychological stress from unmanageable volume of digital notifications (2,847 unread Jira notifications). Contributing Factors: 18-hour work days, 4-5 hours sleep per night, notification anxiety, inability to disconnect from work systems, lack of work-life boundaries. Subject's perfectionist tendencies and inability to ignore notifications created a feedback loop of increasing stress.

Ruling: Work-related fatality. Occupational Safety and Health Administration notified. Case highlights need for digital workplace wellness policies and notification management systems.
